### **Q6: Ethical Considerations in Design** _(8 Marks)_

Ethical design ensures that products contribute positively to society, safeguard user well-being, and respect environmental boundaries.

#### **Key Ethical Considerations:**

1. **User Safety:** Products must meet safety standards and minimize risks (e.g., fireproof materials in appliances).
2. **Data Privacy:** Secure user data with robust encryption and transparent policies.
3. **Inclusivity:** Ensure accessibility for users with diverse needs (e.g., tactile buttons or screen readers).
4. **Sustainability:** Use eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient systems (e.g., recyclable packaging).
5. **Cultural Sensitivity:** Avoid offensive symbols or culturally inappropriate designs.

#### **Importance of Ethical Design:**

- Builds trust and compliance with legal norms.
- Enhances user satisfaction and brand reputation.
- Contributes to societal and environmental betterment.
