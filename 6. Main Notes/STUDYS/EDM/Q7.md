### **Q7: Role of Technology in Modern Product Design** _(8 Marks)_

Technology plays a transformative role in modern product design, enabling innovation, efficiency, and user-centric solutions.

---

#### **Key Roles of Technology in Product Design:**

1. **Enhanced Visualization:**
    
    - Tools like CAD software allow designers to create detailed 3D models.
    - Example: AutoCAD or SolidWorks for precise visual representation.
2. **Rapid Prototyping:**
    
    - Technologies like 3D printing accelerate the prototyping process.
    - Example: Quickly producing prototypes for functional testing.
3. **Simulation and Testing:**
    
    - Software simulates real-world conditions to test performance.
    - Example: Stress analysis using FEA (Finite Element Analysis) tools.
4. **Customization:**
    
    - Advanced manufacturing (e.g., CNC machining) allows for personalized designs.
    - Example: Tailored footwear designs using 3D scanning and printing.
5. **Sustainability:**
    
    - Digital tools optimize material use, reducing waste.
    - Example: AI-driven designs to minimize excess resources.
6. **Collaboration:**
    
    - Cloud-based platforms like Figma enable real-time collaboration among teams globally.

---

#### **Impact of Technology in Design:**

- Increases speed and efficiency in the design process.
- Enhances accuracy and reduces errors.
- Facilitates innovation and competitive differentiation.
- Promotes sustainability and resource efficiency.
