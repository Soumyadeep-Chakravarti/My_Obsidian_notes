### **Q8: Importance of Ergonomics in Product Design** _(8 Marks)_

Ergonomics focuses on designing products that align with human needs, capabilities, and limitations, ensuring comfort, efficiency, and safety.

---

#### **Key Reasons Ergonomics is Important in Product Design:**

1. **User Comfort:**
    
    - Ergonomic designs reduce discomfort and fatigue during prolonged use.
    - Example: Ergonomic chairs provide proper lumbar support.
2. **Increased Productivity:**
    
    - Products designed with ergonomic principles allow users to work efficiently.
    - Example: Adjustable desks improve posture and focus.
3. **Enhanced Safety:**
    
    - Reduces the risk of injuries caused by poor design.
    - Example: Anti-slip handles on tools minimize accidents.
4. **Improved User Experience:**
    
    - Designs tailored to user needs enhance satisfaction and usability.
    - Example: Mobile phones with intuitive button placement.
5. **Universal Accessibility:**
    
    - Inclusive designs accommodate diverse users, including those with disabilities.
    - Example: Lever-style door handles for ease of use by all.
6. **Compliance with Standards:**
    
    - Adhering to ergonomic principles meets legal and industry regulations.

---

#### **Impact of Ergonomics:**

- Boosts user satisfaction and loyalty.
- Minimizes health-related issues, such as repetitive strain injuries.
- Enhances the overall usability and appeal of the product.
