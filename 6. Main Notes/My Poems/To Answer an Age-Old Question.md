To answer an age-old question,  
what whispers in the night,  
what drives us to live,  
and guides us through the fight?

Is it the lure of gold and riches,  
or the power they bestow?  
Is it something darker,  
or something simpler that we know?

Is it the bonds we forge along the way,  
the connections that help us cope?  
Is it the dream of a place to call home,  
or a glimmer of hope we hold close?

Is it the challenge, the climb, the goal,  
or the strength found in each stride?  
Is it the quest for meaning and truth,  
in the ebb and flow of the tide?

Is it the thrill of living fully,  
or the fulfillment that we seek?  
Is it a fleeting fantasy to grasp this answer,  
or so woven in us that we barely see?

Deeply interlaced in every choice we make,  
within the fabric of our days,  
the answer may reside in the journey itself,  
in the subtleties of life’s unspoken ways.
