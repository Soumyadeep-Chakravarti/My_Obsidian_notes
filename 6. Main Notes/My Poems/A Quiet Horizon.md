
The peace of night,  
The neon shine,  
All seems so distant,  
Yet not so mild.

The city hums  
Beneath a sky,  
Where stars once thrived,  
But now they hide.

Reflections blur  
On empty streets,  
A restless calm  
Where silence meets.

The neon flickers,  
Bright but cold,  
A distant fire  
That never holds.

And though it shines,  
It fails to touch  
The peace of night  
We crave so much.

A quiet longing  
Fills the air,  
For something softer,  
Something rare.
