Where, oh where, do you go,  
When you're hurt, to whom do you turn?  
When there's no one, where do you lie,  
When there's no place to rest, do you cry?

Or do you keep moving,  
Through the rust of tired, winding ways,  
Hoping the night will carry you,  
Until the dawn breaks through the haze?

Do you whisper to the stars above,  
Cold and distant in their light?  
Or do you wander, worn but steady,  
Through the endless stretch of night?

Do you search for light ahead,  
Or become your own flame,  
Burning it all, all away,  
Back into the darkness from where you came?

Tell me, does the pain fade away,  
Or do we grow numb to its sting?  
Does breath grow calm,  
Or do we still suffocate, struggling within?

Do we bleed for the dreams we chased,  
Or are they forgotten and lost,  
Swallowed into the void,  
Into the nothingness we've crossed?

So tell me, my soul, my spirit, my own nemesis,  
Where do you flee when you're torn?  
When there's no one left, where do you rest?  
When you find no peace, do you mourn,  
Or do you simply fade away,  
Ceasing to exist, with nothing more to say?
