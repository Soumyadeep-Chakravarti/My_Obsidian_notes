### **Slide 6: Cons of Array Processors**

- **Limited Flexibility**
    - Specialized design for specific tasks
- **Complex Programming**
    - Challenges in developing and optimizing software
- **Cost and Resource Intensive**
    - High hardware costs and potential power consumption
- **Communication Overhead**
    - Potential bottlenecks in data transfer between PEs
- **Underutilization in Non-Parallel Tasks**
    - Inefficiency in tasks that are not easily parallelized
